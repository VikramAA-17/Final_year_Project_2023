About Us

Farm Frndz is a web app that predicts the crop to be yielded and fertilizers to the predicted crop. Here are the following things provided by the web app.

  • Farmers can predict crop from pH value

  • Basic nutrient fertilizers are also predicted

  • Naive users can aslo use the web app

Our services

pH Prediction

The farmer needs to perform a manual soil test with litmus paper and distilled water. Upload the image of litmus paper to determine the pH of the soil.

Predict

Crop Prediction

Once the pH is determined from the image,the farmer can use the pH value as input and predict the required crop.

Predict



Fertilizer Prediction

If the pH value and crop is known to the farmer, he/she can give them as inputs to determine the NPK values and predict the amount of basic nutrient fertilizers to be used for the production.
